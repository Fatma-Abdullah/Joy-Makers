Meet us

Joy Makers Foundation is an integrated Saudi institution where it organizes events whether parties, corporate events or even social events. The market leader of the joy-makers, known for its strong focus on service excellence, innovation, responsiveness, flexibility, customer service quality, and specialized skills.

Our Vision

We aim to be the first destination in Saudi Arabia, a leading provider of all services in the area of organization and planning by enabling technology and human resources to understand and meet the needs and desires of our customers.

Our Mission

Optimism, innovation and perseverance are at the core of an organization's culture. We really believe that anything can be done because of creativity, innovation and planning. We believe it is our job to stay modern in the event industry, watch new products, and be thought leaders. Only then can we be at our best and keep your events new. "We are the experts in creating events that inspire, motivate and impress! Through attractive design, creating new experiences, awakening the senses, and evoking emotion. "

What do we offer in the Joy Makers?

When you choose "Joy Makers", you choose excellence. We are specialized and know that ideas come from anywhere and everywhere, so we are open to diverse views. Our team has the power and inspiration to think about events in a variety of different and creative ways.

Why are we special?

We rely on a unique balance between technology and concern for our people; to ensure that each of our services is of the highest quality. Our dedicated team works round the clock to find and test the best products.
